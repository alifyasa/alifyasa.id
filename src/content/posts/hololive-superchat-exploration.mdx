## Data Gathering & Preparation

I scraped Hololyzer's superchat data ([https://hololyzer.net/youtube/](https://hololyzer.net/youtube/)). The dataset can be accessed from Google Drive at `superchats/2024.csv`.

I first substituted non-Latin channel names with their Latin counterparts. Then I parsed the superchats themselves, separating the currency and amount.

I encountered a problem when trying to convert the currency to USD; I found out that there are no easy ways to obtain exchange rates for a certain date. I have to either pay for an API service or manually download from sites like [https://investing.com/](https://investing.com/). Here are some interesting services I found:

1. [https://github.com/fawazahmed0/exchange-api](https://github.com/fawazahmed0/exchange-api)
2. [https://www.exchangerate-api.com/docs/free](https://www.exchangerate-api.com/docs/free)

In the end, I gave up and just used the latest exchange rates.
